The Research Division

Our central research outpost is stationed on Polyphemus, within the Messier 82 Galaxy. The facility monitors local ecosystems, catalogues newly discovered species, and analyzes the bioluminescent lifeforms that inhabit its oceans and crystalline deserts.

Our scientists, explorers, and archivists work tirelessly to decode the biology, behavior, and histories of intelligent species across the known galaxies. Every discovery is meticulously documented, ensuring that knowledge once scattered among the stars becomes accessible to all who seek to understand the vast diversity of life.
