Our position

About our leader

Yu Xiang, the president of The Hyperion Research Institute, is recognized as a leading figure in the exploration and study of extraterrestrial species.
Known for extending the boundaries of scientific knowledge and pioneering research into alien lifeforms, he has guided the institute to become a hub of innovation and discovery across the galaxy.
With a passion for uncovering the unknown,it is ensured that the institute not only catalogues species but also understands their ecosystems and behaviors. Under Yu Xiang's leadership,Hyperion Research Institute continues to inspire scientists and explorers across the universe
